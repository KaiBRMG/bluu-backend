## 🔄 How We Work

Bluu Rock manages the day-to-day operations of your account, including chatting, sales strategy, content scheduling, and performance optimisation.

To keep everything running smoothly, we rely on timely content delivery, approvals, and open communication. Clear collaboration allows us to scale your earnings efficiently and consistently.

## 📌 Your Dashboard

This is a multi-layered workspace designed to seamlessly connect you with management and our chat team, providing real-time updates on custom requests, calls, items, and content requirements. To ensure a smooth and efficient workflow, we kindly ask that card statuses are updated accurately and in a timely manner. This allows our team to coordinate effectively, release content as scheduled, and maintain momentum—ultimately supporting consistent earnings growth and fostering strong, lasting relationships with your subscribers.

- 📸 **Custom Requests** 
These are unique, custom goods your fans request from you at a much higher rate than general pay-per-view (PPV) content. These can come in the form of pictures, videos, calls, and items. Once you are done with a particular video, call, or item, mark it as **Completed**.

- 🖼️ **Content Plan**
We follow a structured approach to planning and scheduling content. To maintain your account and drive up your earnings, we require various content, such PPV’s, timeline posts, social media posts (SFW), campaign content, etc. These will show in your Content Plan. Please aim to fulfil the requirements by the due date as this in accordance with our planned schedule. Once you see a new content requirement, please mark it as **In Progress** so we know you have seen it and working on it — and once you are done, mark it as **Completed**.

- 🔗 **Google Drive Upload Link**
Find your Google Drive upload link. This is where you can upload the content you recorded — custom requests, content requirements, etc. Please make sure to upload in the appropriate folder. Feel free to create a new folder or rename the file.
